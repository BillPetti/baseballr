# **MLB Game Endpoint Overview**

- [`mlb_game_info()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_info.md):
  Retrieve additional game information for major and minor league games.

- [`mlb_game_pks()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_pks.md):
  Get MLB Game Info by Date and Level.

- [`mlb_game_content()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_content.md):
  Retrieve additional game content for major and minor league games.

- [`mlb_game_linescore()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_linescore.md):
  Retrieve game linescores for major and minor league games.

- [`mlb_game_wp()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_wp.md):
  Acquire win probability for Major and Minor League games.

- [`mlb_game_pace()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_pace.md):
  Retrieve game pace metrics for major and minor league.

- [`mlb_game_changes()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_changes.md):
  Acquire time codes for Major and Minor League games.

- [`mlb_game_context_metrics()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_game_context_metrics.md):
  Acquire game context metrics for Major and Minor League games.

- [`mlb_pbp()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_pbp.md):
  Acquire pitch-by-pitch data for Major and Minor League games.

- [`mlb_pbp_diff()`](https://billpetti.github.io/baseballr/reference/mlb_pbp_diff.md):
  Acquire pitch-by-pitch data between two timecodes for Major and Minor
  League games.

## Details

### **MLB Game**

These functions retrieve MLB game information, content, linescores, win
probability, pace, changes, context metrics, and pitch-by-pitch data
from the MLB Stats API.
